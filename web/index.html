How it works

Send the URL as plain text to the `/` endpoint. We validate it, hash it, and store it alongside a compact identifier. Visiting that identifier redirects to your original location.

  1. Validate and sanitize your URL
  2. Generate a collision-safe ID
  3. Store it in NeDB and respond with the ID
